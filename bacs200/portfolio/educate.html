TED Talks

This could be why you're depressed or anxious - Johann Hari

  • Johann speaks about his personal experience with depression.
  • He was prescribed medication to treat his depression and he felt great for a while.That is until they didn't work anymore.
  • Johann states that he was depressed because he was lonely. We tend to isolate ourselves, avoid others, or simply can't find people we want to be around.
  • Humans need important connection with one another, and it is vital that we find people we can rely on to fulfill our happiness.

Being an Introvert is a Good Thing - Crystal Robello

  • Yes, introverts are shy and many believe that we are unable to proudly share our ideas.
  • Crystal states that 50% of the American population are considered introverts.
  • We don't have to change who we are to be succesful, respected, and creative!

It is okay not to have a plan - Mithila Palkar

  • Why do we worry so much about the future if it hasn't even happened yet?
  • It's okay to not have a plan for every step of our life! Everything will fall into place.
  • Mithila states that we should not let others dictate our lives; do what YOU want with YOUR life.
