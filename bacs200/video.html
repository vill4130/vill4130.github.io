Value Proposition

Educating + Empowering Voters to Exercise Their Rights

Compelling Vision:

Founded in 1920 in Chicago, Illinois, The League of Women Voters' focuses on educating and empowering voters whilst defending democracy. In 1928, the Colorado chapter was formed and have since continued to make an impact on goverment issues (19 active leagues in colorado). Because of their in-depth research, studies, and discussions, the League of Women Voters' are a non-partisan group that provide facts and reason when presenting information on voting issues.

Notable achievments include:

  • Automatic Voter Registration (2019)
  • Amendments Y & Z, establishing bipartisan redistricting commissions following each census to combat gerrymandering (2018)
  • Restored Voting Rights for Parolees (2019)
  • National Popular Vote Interstate Compact (2019)
  • Equal Pay for Equal Work (2019)

Strategies:

  • By promoting inclusion, equity, and diversity, The League of Women Voters' is an orginization that blocks all barriers to exclusion of of people on the basis of gender, gender identitiy, etnicity, race, origin, age, sexual orientation, culture, religion, etc.
  • The league actively writes guides regarding public policy to inform voters on varying issues with facts and no bias.
  • The league fights for what is right and puts in the effort to crush voter suppression. This solidifies their mission of empowering voters and defending democracy

The League of Women Voters'

The League of Women Voters', a non-partisan group, focuses on educating people and empowering voters while defending democracy. In 1928, the Colorado chapter was formed, and they have since continued to make an impact on government issues.

The Colorado chapter has accomplished amazing things such as restoring voting rights for parolees, push for automatic voter registration, and preventing politicians from buying their own election. Be sure to check out their site and click on the “donate today” button on my page to support this wonderful organization.

Resources

During this incredibly stressful election season, it is Because of groups like The League of Women Voters’, that people have access to facts regarding voting issues. The in-depth research, studies, and discussions the League provide ensure that voters have access to credible information.

The League has an awesome resource called Vote411.org that provides election information tailored to your location. Whether the election is local, state, or Federal, Vote411.org provides information for you to make knowledgeable decisions, confirm your polling location, and see what your personal ballot will look like.
